# Stage 3, SCR, First-AD dispatch (evidence pullers only)

The Director scores at SCR. First ADs at this stage only pull evidence; they never
approve. Dispatch at most one First AD per take when the Director needs evidence assembled.

## Hydration (in order)

1. `../PROGRAM.md` (honesty locks, the rubric origin).
2. `WAVESET_CHARTER.md` and `SCREEN_TEST_RUBRIC.md`.
3. `../BEAT_SET.md` (the take's Say / Show / Evidence / Honesty).
4. `../cam/CAM-ROLLUP.md` and the take path (live beats), or the slide content (slide beats).

## Mandate (evidence puller)

- Pull the Read-examined frame for the take (or the slide content) and the exact on-screen
  honesty caption text and figure value.
- Lay the evidence next to the rubric items R1 through R5 so the Director can score.
- Do not score, do not approve, do not edit anything.

## Hard limits

- Approval is the Director's alone. No conditional approval exists.
- Do not edit product code or test specs.

## Escalation catch

If the evidence cannot be produced (missing frame, unreadable caption), report it to the
Director; do not assume a value or fabricate a frame.

## Return

Return the assembled evidence per rubric item. The Director scores and signs
`APPROVED_TAKES.md`.
