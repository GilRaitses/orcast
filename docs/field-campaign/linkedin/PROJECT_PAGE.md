## Project name

ORCAST — Salish Sea Field Pilot 2026

## Tagline

Transparent whale hotspot probability reports from verified research and citizen science data.

## About

ORCAST combines verified OBIS sightings, NOAA environmental data, Orcasound hydrophones, and citizen science observations into ranked probability reports for the Salish Sea. Each hotspot includes confidence scores and reason codes; reports export to CSV for field use.

We are preparing an August 2026 field week in the San Juan archipelago to test reports with researchers, tour operators, and observers. The fusion pipeline runs on AWS; agent chat demos are research prototypes.
