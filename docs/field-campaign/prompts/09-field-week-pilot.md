# Field week pilot plan

## Purpose

Document what the August field week tests and what feedback ORCAST needs.

## Narrative

During the last week of August 2026, ORCAST will test probability reports with observers, naturalists, and research partners in the San Juan archipelago. Success metrics include: partners can generate a report without assistance, CSV output is usable on a boat, hotspot rankings match local expert intuition often enough to invite iteration, and offline cached demo works on spotty cell service.

Feedback forms capture: clarity of reason codes, missing data sources, desired map layers, and partnership interest.
