## Family summary

The knowledge graph and scientific provenance corpus is directly relevant and strong. The SciERC / SciIE paper (1808.09602) introduces a multi-task framework that jointly identifies entities, relations, and coreference clusters in scientific articles — the foundational NLP substrate for building the Claim/Method/Experiment graph structure described in orcast's provenance contract. The SciClaim paper (2109.10453) goes further: it builds a graph annotation schema that captures fine-grained experimental associations at the claim-span level with entity nodes and typed relational edges, exactly the C/M/X binding model orcast's ProvenanceGraph implements. MindSearch (2407.20183) demonstrates the multi-agent query-decomposition + knowledge graph construction approach at scale: it decomposes complex queries into a directed acyclic graph of sub-questions and synthesizes answers across web pages with explicit linkage — the graph-as-answer-structure pattern. The knowledge graph generation from scientific literature paper (2011.01103) establishes that NLP-based extraction of entities and relations from scientific text into knowledge graphs is feasible and improves research navigation, supporting the claim that C/M/X graph construction from step logs is a tractable system design. The MindMap paper (2308.09729) directly demonstrates that knowledge graph prompting reduces LLM hallucination and improves reasoning transparency by forcing responses to be grounded in structured graph nodes — the same property that makes orcast's annotation-backed provenance graph more honest than unstructured LLM narration. The grounding dialogue systems paper (2103.16289) establishes KG-aware decoding as an architectural pattern for knowledge-grounded generation, precedent for the orcast pattern of narrating from graph-structured skill outputs.

**Verdict: Supported** — C/M/X graph schemas for scientific provenance and span-bound citation binding are established in the literature. Primary citations: 2109.10453 (SciClaim fine-grained C/M/X schema), 1808.09602 (SciIE joint entity-relation extraction), 2407.20183 (MindSearch graph-of-queries), 2308.09729 (MindMap KG-grounded reasoning).
