# ORCAST Product Narrative (2026 submission framing)

Source of truth for the shifted product philosophy. Submission documents are
updated against this. Operator-framed 2026-06-28.

## One-line

ORCAST is a two-sided loop around Salish Sea killer whales. Encounter forecasting
is the grounding layer. An AI orchestration console serves three parties at once,
the tourists who visit, the researchers who study whale behavior, and the whales
themselves.

## The loop

Encounter forecasting is not the product. It is the grounding layer that both
sides stand on, and each side improves it.

### A side, the visitor console (public)

Encounter forecasting grounds real trip use cases. A visitor states intent in
the console. The console transduces that intent into planning objects, the trip
panels and routes the visitor acts on. Different trip scenarios share the same
grounding forecast, for example a visit, a kayak outing, a ferry crossing, or a
shore-based watch. The forecast is the substrate the planning objects are built
on, not a separate feature.

### B side, the research workbench

A behavior-analysis research workbench. Collaborative review of community reports
and annotation of hydrophone detections run today, a modeled terrain and
bathymetry 3D twin lives in the research sandbox, and the build is extending
toward dtag modeling replay. The workbench
exists to close a gap that advances both sides of the loop. Better behavior
analysis sharpens the forecast that grounds the visitor trips, and the visitor
activity and the twin give the research side more to work with.

### The bridge, AI managed orchestration

An AI layer orchestrates the routines across both sides. It is an implementation
of the Friend console from the pax project, the liquid-glass research console
tracked in the LGC lane. It is set up for the best shared benefit across the
parties, conservation, enjoyment, and rigor, for the tourists visiting the Salish
Sea, the researchers studying whale behavior, and the whales themselves.

## What changed from the prior framing

- The headline is the two-sided loop and the shared-benefit orchestration, not a
  single forecast accuracy claim.
- Encounter forecasting is described as the grounding layer under trips and under
  research, rather than the end product.
- The console is framed as intent transduction into planning objects.
- The terrain and bathymetry 3D twin and the dtag and annotation tools are framed
  as the research side that closes the loop.
- The pax Friend console (LGC) is named as the AI orchestration layer serving all
  three stakeholders.

## Honesty locks that do not change

The forecast is a modeled probability, not an observed sighting. Detections carry
a confidence score, not confirmed ground truth. Probability zones are predictions,
not guarantees. The 3D twin is modeled, not measured. No submission copy may
weaken these disclaimers.
