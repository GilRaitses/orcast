INTEGRATING the existing modules — do not invent placeholder meshes or controls.

## Port these modules into your Bash game app (integrate, do not npm-run orcast web)
- web/lib/scene/orca/ — loadOrcaMesh, OrcaRig, biologging driveOrca, materials
- web/lib/scene/orcaStrike/ — controls, pilotStateMachine, breach, blowhole, scoring, match, islands
- web/lib/scene/orcaPilot/ — deadReckoning, chaseCamera (do NOT edit input.ts semantics; use orcaStrike/controls for Q/E)
- web/lib/scene/boats/ — spawnBoats, spawnKayaks, BoatMarker, KayakMarker, collision, sink
- web/lib/scene/sonar/ — ping, teleport, radar
- web/app/(game)/orca-strike/ — reference integration (OrcaStrikeScene.tsx, lobby, HUD)

## Hard gates
- Load orca.glb skinned mesh + DTAG driver — NEVER substitute polygon orca
- Q dive, E surface, A/D roll, W/S thrust (read STRIKE-controls.md)
- Boats AND kayaks in scene
- O-key plays hydrophone m4a slice
- Tileset: https://d8kxxpcnj3ub5.cloudfront.net/3dtwin/full/tileset.json
